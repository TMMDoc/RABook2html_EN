FluxBend selects your tools fully automatically according to your tool inventory and preference. Nevertheless it is important that you keep the size of a appropriate tool in mind. The more the tool differs from the ideal tool, the worse your accuracy will be. A general overview of appropriate tools could be found in the Trumpf tool catalog.
