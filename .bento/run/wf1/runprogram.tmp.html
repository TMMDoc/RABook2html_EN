In this environment, the press-brake is being used to perform the bending. Each bend essentially consists of two phases:

  1. Tap the foot pedal to move the back-gauges into position.

  2. The sheet is positioned into the brake, and press and hold the foot pedal to perform the bend.

The indicators on the top left guide you through this cycle.

The 3D display on the right displays how the blank should be inserted into the machine for each step. As you go through the operations, this picture changes to display the appropriate bend. Since this is a live 3D view, you can use the display button to change what is displayed, or to change the viewpoint. You can also use the zoom and pan navigation buttons along the bottom of the screen to manipulate the 3D view.
